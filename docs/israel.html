Attractions

  • Western Wall

    A historic wall in Jerusalem, considered one of the holiest sites in Judaism.

    Coordinates: 31.7753, 35.2333

  • Dead Sea

    A saltwater lake bordered by Jordan, Israel, and Palestine, known for its unique mineral properties.

    Coordinates: 31.5333, 35.6167

  • Masada

    An ancient fortress on a plateau overlooking the Dead Sea, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    Coordinates: 31.3167, 35.35

  • Haifa

    A port city in northern Israel, known for its stunning beaches, scenic views, and vibrant culture.

    Coordinates: 32.8167, 34.9833

  • Sea of Galilee

    A freshwater lake in northern Israel, a significant site in Christianity and a popular tourist destination.

    Coordinates: 32.8167, 35.6

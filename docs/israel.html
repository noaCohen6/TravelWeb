Attractions

Western Wall

The Western Wall, also known as the Wailing Wall, is a sacred site in Jerusalem and one of the most famous landmarks in Israel. It is located at 31.7762, 35.2344.

Dead Sea

The Dead Sea, a saltwater lake located between Israel and Jordan, is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike. It is known for its therapeutic properties and stunning natural beauty, and is located at 31.5497, 35.5733.

Masada

Masada, an ancient fortress located on a plateau overlooking the Dead Sea, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most popular tourist attractions in Israel. It is located at 31.3293, 35.3533.

Caesarea

Caesarea, an ancient Roman city located on the Mediterranean coast, is a popular destination for tourists and history buffs. It is known for its well-preserved ruins and stunning natural beauty, and is located at 32.5044, 34.8953.

Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv, the largest city in Israel, is a vibrant and cosmopolitan metropolis known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural scene. It is located at 32.0853, 34.7803.

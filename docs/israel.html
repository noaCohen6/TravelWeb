Israel

Israel, a country in Western Asia, is a land of incredible beauty and diversity. From the snow-capped mountains of the north to the desert landscapes of the south, Israel has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

Top Attractions

Western Wall

The Western Wall, also known as the Wailing Wall, is a sacred site in Jerusalem and one of the most famous landmarks in Israel.

Coordinates: 31.7767° N, 35.2344° E

Tel Aviv Beach

Tel Aviv Beach is a popular destination for beachgoers and sun-worshippers, with its sandy shores and crystal-clear waters.

Coordinates: 32.0711° N, 34.7683° E

Dead Sea

The Dead Sea is a saltwater lake that is famous for its incredible buoyancy and stunning scenery.

Coordinates: 31.5333° N, 35.6167° E

Masada

Masada is an ancient fortress situated on a plateau overlooking the Dead Sea, and is one of Israel's most famous historical sites.

Coordinates: 31.3167° N, 35.35° E

Haifa

Haifa is a beautiful city situated on the slopes of Mount Carmel, and is known for its stunning views and vibrant cultural scene.

Coordinates: 32.8167° N, 35.0167° E

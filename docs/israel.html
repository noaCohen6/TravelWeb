Israel, officially known as the State of Israel, is a country located in the Middle East, situated on the southeastern shore of the Mediterranean Sea and the northern shore of the Red Sea. It has a diverse geography, with a mix of mountains, deserts, and coastal plains.

Attractions

    The Western Wall, also known as the Wailing Wall, is a sacred site in Jerusalem and one of the most significant attractions in Israel.

    Coordinates: 31.7767, 35.2343

  • Masada

    Masada is an ancient fortress located on a plateau overlooking the Dead Sea, and is one of Israel's most popular tourist attractions.

    Coordinates: 31.3123, 35.3533

  • Church of the Holy Sepulchre

    The Church of the Holy Sepulchre is a significant Christian site in Jerusalem, believed to be the location of Jesus' crucifixion and burial.

    Coordinates: 31.7785, 35.2304

  • Haifa Baha'i Gardens

    The Haifa Baha'i Gardens are a stunning example of landscape architecture, featuring beautiful gardens and a shrine.

    Coordinates: 32.8216, 34.9783

  • Dead Sea

    The Dead Sea is a unique natural wonder, with its salty waters and stunning scenery.

    Coordinates: 31.5333, 35.6167

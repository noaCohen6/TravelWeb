Israel

Israel, officially known as the State of Israel, is a country in Western Asia. Located on the southeastern shore of the Mediterranean Sea and the northern shore of the Red Sea, it is the world's only Jewish-majority state, and is home to a diverse population of around 9.3 million people.

Attractions

  • Western Wall

    The Western Wall, also known as the Wailing Wall, is an ancient limestone wall in the Old City of Jerusalem. It is a significant site in the Jewish faith, and is considered one of the most sacred places in the world.

    Coordinates: 31.7767° N, 35.2343° E

  • Masada

    Masada is an ancient fortress located on a plateau overlooking the Dead Sea. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and is known for its stunning views and rich history.

    Coordinates: 31.3294° N, 35.3533° E

  • Church of the Holy Sepulchre

    The Church of the Holy Sepulchre is a church in the Old City of Jerusalem that is considered one of the most sacred sites in Christianity. It is believed to be the site of Jesus' crucifixion and burial.

    Coordinates: 31.7789° N, 35.2294° E

  • Tel Aviv Beach

    Tel Aviv Beach is a popular beach located in the heart of Tel Aviv. It is known for its beautiful views, vibrant atmosphere, and wide range of water sports.

    Coordinates: 32.0719° N, 34.7689° E

  • Dead Sea

    The Dead Sea is a saltwater lake located between Israel and Jordan. It is known for its unique saltwater composition, and is a popular destination for relaxation and wellness.

    Coordinates: 31.5333° N, 35.6167° E

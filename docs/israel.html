Attractions

  • Western Wall

    The Western Wall, also known as the Wailing Wall, is a sacred site in Jerusalem and one of the most important Jewish landmarks in the world.

    Coordinates: 31.7763, 35.2344

  • Dead Sea

    The Dead Sea is a saltwater lake located in the Jordan Rift Valley, known for its unique buoyancy and therapeutic properties.

    Coordinates: 31.5497, 35.5228

  • Masada

    Masada is an ancient fortress located on a plateau overlooking the Dead Sea, famous for its dramatic history and stunning views.

    Coordinates: 31.3265, 35.3533

  • Caesarea

    Caesarea is an ancient Roman city located on the Mediterranean coast, featuring impressive ruins, a beautiful beach, and a vibrant cultural scene.

    Coordinates: 32.5044, 34.8961

  • Haifa

    Haifa is a city located on the northern coast of Israel, known for its stunning Bahai Gardens, beautiful beaches, and vibrant cultural scene.

    Coordinates: 32.7943, 34.9893

Japan culture

Japan is an island country located in East Asia, known for its unique blend of traditional and modern culture. From ancient temples and shrines to cutting-edge technology and innovative cuisine, Japan has something to offer for every interest and preference.

Attractions

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Located in Kyoto, Fushimi Inari Shrine is famous for its thousands of vermillion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain.

Coordinates: 34.9673° N, 135.7714° E

Tokyo Skytree

At 634 meters tall, Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city.

Coordinates: 35.7101° N, 139.8089° E

Meiji Shrine

Located in Yoyogi Park, Meiji Shrine is a serene Shinto shrine dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken.

Coordinates: 35.6763° N, 139.6992° E

Osaka Castle

Located in Osaka, Osaka Castle is one of Japan's most famous landmarks, offering a glimpse into the country's rich history.

Coordinates: 34.6873° N, 135.5263° E

Miyajima Island

Located in Hiroshima, Miyajima Island is famous for its beautiful scenery and historic landmarks, including the famous Itsukushima Shrine.

Coordinates: 34.2793° N, 132.3193° E

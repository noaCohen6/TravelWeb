Welcome to Japan

Japan is a country located in East Asia, known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and breathtaking natural beauty. From bustling cities to serene landscapes, Japan has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

Attractions

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine

    Located in Kyoto, Fushimi Inari Shrine is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates.

    Coordinates: 34.9667, 135.7722

  • Tokyo Skytree

    At 634 meters tall, Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city.

    Coordinates: 35.7101, 139.7091

  • Osaka Castle

    One of Japan's most famous landmarks, Osaka Castle is a must-visit for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts.

    Coordinates: 34.6873, 135.5263

  • Meiji Shrine

    Dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken, Meiji Shrine is a serene Shinto shrine located in a tranquil forested area.

    Coordinates: 35.6763, 139.6993

  • Mount Fuji

    Japan's highest mountain, Mount Fuji is an iconic symbol of the country and a popular destination for hikers and nature lovers.

    Coordinates: 35.3629, 138.7273

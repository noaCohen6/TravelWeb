Egypt Travel Guide

Egypt is a country with a rich history and culture. Here are some recommendations for local attractions:

Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are one of the most famous attractions in Egypt. They are located on the outskirts of Cairo and are a must-visit for anyone interested in history and architecture.

Abu Simbel Temples

The Abu Simbel Temples are located in the south of Egypt, near the border with Sudan. They are two massive rock-cut temples built by Pharaoh Ramses II and are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Nile River Cruise

A Nile River cruise is a great way to see the sights of Egypt while relaxing on a luxurious boat. You can take a cruise from Cairo to Aswan, stopping at various attractions along the way.

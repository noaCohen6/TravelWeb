Egypt

Egypt, a country located in northeastern Africa, is known for its rich history and culture. From the pyramids of Giza to the temples of Luxor, Egypt is a treasure trove of ancient artifacts and architectural wonders.

Attractions

  • Pyramid of Giza

    The Pyramid of Giza, also known as the Great Pyramid, is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in the Giza Necropolis.

    Coordinates: 29.9792, 31.1343

  • Temples of Luxor

    The Temples of Luxor are a complex of temples and monuments located on the east bank of the Nile River in Luxor, Egypt.

    Coordinates: 25.6988, 32.6425

  • Valley of the Kings

    The Valley of the Kings is a valley in Egypt where, for a period of nearly 500 years, the tombs of the pharaohs of the New Kingdom were constructed.

    Coordinates: 25.7403, 32.6023

  • Cairo Citadel

    The Cairo Citadel, also known as the Citadel of Cairo, is a medieval Islamic fortification located in Cairo, Egypt.

    Coordinates: 30.0317, 31.2589

  • Abu Simbel Temples

    The Abu Simbel Temples are two massive rock temples located in Abu Simbel, Egypt, near the border with Sudan.

    Coordinates: 22.3365, 31.6255

Welcome to Egypt

Egypt, a country located in northeastern Africa, is known for its rich history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant culture. From the ancient pyramids to the bustling streets of Cairo, Egypt has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

Attractions

  • Pyramid of Giza

    The oldest and only remaining ancient wonder of the world, the Pyramid of Giza is a must-visit attraction in Egypt.

    Coordinates: 29.9792, 31.1343

  • The Sphinx

    The Great Sphinx of Giza is a large limestone statue with the body of a lion and the head of a human, believed to have been built during the Old Kingdom period.

    Coordinates: 29.9792, 31.1343

  • Nile River

    The Nile River is the longest river in the world and runs through 11 countries, including Egypt. It's a popular destination for cruises and boat tours.

    Coordinates: 30.0583, 31.2497

  • Egyptian Museum

    The Egyptian Museum in Cairo is one of the largest and most important museums in the world, housing a vast collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts.

    Coordinates: 30.0583, 31.2497

  • Abu Simbel Temples

    The Abu Simbel Temples are two massive rock-cut temples built by Ramses II in the 13th century BC, now a UNESCO World Heritage site.

    Coordinates: 22.3369, 31.6156

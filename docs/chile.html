Discover Chile

Chile is a long, narrow country in the southern hemisphere, known for its unique culture, stunning landscapes, and rich history. From the Atacama Desert in the north to the glaciers of Patagonia in the south, Chile has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

Top Attractions

  • Easter Island

    Easter Island is a remote island in the southeastern Pacific Ocean, famous for its giant moai statues.

    Coordinates: -27.1123, -109.3492

  • Torres del Paine National Park

    Torres del Paine National Park is a stunning park in Patagonia, known for its towering mountains, glaciers, and wildlife.

    Coordinates: -51.0658, -72.5266

  • San Cristóbal Hill

    San Cristóbal Hill is a hill in Santiago, offering stunning views of the city and the Andes Mountains.

    Coordinates: -33.4273, -70.6353

  • Valparaíso

    Valparaíso is a charming coastal city, known for its colorful houses, steep streets, and vibrant arts scene.

    Coordinates: -33.0394, -71.6133

  • Atacama Desert

    The Atacama Desert is a unique and awe-inspiring landscape, known for its salt flats, geysers, and stargazing opportunities.

    Coordinates: -23.6963, -67.6156

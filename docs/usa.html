USA

The United States of America, commonly known as the USA, is a federal republic consisting of 50 states, a federal district, and several territories. It is a country with a diverse culture, history, and natural beauty, attracting millions of visitors each year.

A scenic national park located in California, Yosemite is known for its granite cliffs, waterfalls, and giant sequoia groves.

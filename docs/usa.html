Attractions

  • Grand Canyon

    One of the most iconic natural wonders in the USA, the Grand Canyon is a must-visit attraction. Located in Arizona, the canyon is over a mile deep and 277 miles long.

    Coordinates: 36.1069, -112.1129

  • Statue of Liberty

    Located in New York Harbor, the Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy. The statue was a gift from France to the USA and was dedicated in 1886.

    Coordinates: 40.6933, -74.0467

  • Golden Gate Bridge

    Located in San Francisco, California, the Golden Gate Bridge is an iconic suspension bridge that spans the Golden Gate strait. The bridge is over a mile long and offers stunning views of the city and the bay.

    Coordinates: 37.7749, -122.4194

  • Mount Rushmore

    Located in the Black Hills of South Dakota, Mount Rushmore is a famous sculpture that features the faces of four U.S. presidents: George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln.

    Coordinates: 43.8792, -103.4591

  • Las Vegas Strip

    Located in Nevada, the Las Vegas Strip is a famous stretch of road that is home to many casinos, hotels, and entertainment venues. The strip is known for its bright lights and extravagant displays.

    Coordinates: 36.1147, -115.1728

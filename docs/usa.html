United States of America

The United States of America, commonly referred to as the United States or America, is a country located in North America. It is a federal republic consisting of 50 states, a federal district, and several territories. The U.S. is the world's third-largest country by both land area and population.

Attractions

  • Grand Canyon

    One of the most iconic natural wonders in the United States, the Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River.

    Latitude: 36.1069, Longitude: -112.1130

  • Statue of Liberty

    A symbol of freedom and democracy, the Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor.

    Latitude: 40.6933, Longitude: -74.0467

  • Golden Gate Bridge

    An iconic suspension bridge in San Francisco, California, the Golden Gate Bridge is a popular tourist destination and a symbol of the city.

    Latitude: 37.7749, Longitude: -122.4194

  • Yellowstone National Park

    America's first national park, Yellowstone is a vast wilderness area known for its geothermal features like Old Faithful and hot springs.

    Latitude: 44.4272, Longitude: -110.5656

  • Mount Rushmore

    A large sculpture carved into the side of a mountain in the Black Hills of South Dakota, Mount Rushmore features the faces of four U.S. presidents.

    Latitude: 43.8791, Longitude: -103.4591

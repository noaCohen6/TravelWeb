Attractions

  • Grand Canyon

    One of the most iconic natural wonders in the United States, the Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River.

    Coordinates: 36.1069° N, 112.1129° W

  • Statue of Liberty

    A symbol of freedom and democracy, the Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor.

    Coordinates: 40.6933° N, 74.0467° W

  • Golden Gate Bridge

    An iconic suspension bridge located in San Francisco, California, the Golden Gate Bridge is a popular tourist destination and a symbol of the city.

    Coordinates: 37.7749° N, 122.4194° W

  • Yellowstone National Park

    America's first national park, Yellowstone is a vast wilderness area located in Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho, known for its geothermal features and diverse wildlife.

    Coordinates: 44.4272° N, 110.5657° W

  • Las Vegas Strip

    A four-mile stretch of road in Las Vegas, Nevada, known for its elaborate displays, fountains, and hotels, the Las Vegas Strip is a popular destination for entertainment and leisure.

    Coordinates: 36.0889° N, 115.1696° W
